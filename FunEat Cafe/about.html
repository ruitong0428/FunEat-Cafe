FunEat Cafe History

FunEat Cafe started operating in 2010, and its founder is Rui Tong. She is still the owner of the restaurant, and now she is 20 years old. The reason she opened the FunEat Cafe is to let more people eat delicious food and donate the money earned to more people in need or disaster victims.



FunEat Cafe Construction Processs

FunEat Cafe was originally built with glass walls because of the good lighting, but considering the presence of children and other reasons, she was afraid that it would scratch people when broken, so she decided to use acrylic walls. In order to ensure the freshness of the ingredients, she also bought the best refrigerator and purchased a range hood to prevent the spread of oil smoke. The toilet and FunEat Cafe floors are made of non-slip material. The toilets are cleaned by staff every day to ensure cleanliness.
